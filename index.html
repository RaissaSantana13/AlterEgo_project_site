Alter Ego

Alter Ego é um álbum da artista tailandesa Lisa, que faz parte do grupo de kpop Blackpink. Neste album a artista explora 5 alter egos, chamados respectivamente Roxi, Sunni, Kiki, Speedi e Vixi.

Roxi

I’m a Rockstar, baby I’m a Rockstar and you, you can call me Roxi. Sit back and enjoy the entertainment of your life. If you know me you know I live and breathe this lifestyle. Follow me and I’ll give you a glimpse into my world. Music is all around me because it is me. I escape into a world that is electrifying with every stroke of my guitar. This is why I live, it’s to entertain always command the stage.

Sunni

No time to waste, so Kiss me! Bonjour, I’m Sunni—charmed, I’m sure. I have a certain je ne sais quoi for sun-kissed days and sea-breezy nights. The beach is my sanctuary, where the golden rays warm my skin and the whispers of the ocean fill me with endless inspiration. There’s just something irresistible about the way the waves call my name—don’t you think?

Kiki

If you’re looking for me, I’m probably burning CDs or finding something new to obsess over. I live for the thrill—whether it’s the perfect playlist, a late-night adventure, or just something that we can chill and chat over like a gameboard. Page me, call me, AIM me—just don't leave me on read, hit me up if you wanna “Kiki”! 🖤☆

Speedi

Speedi here!—Straight drifting your way. Built for the rush, wired for the win. Hop into to this fast track that’s sure to keep you in this ride. I’ll give you a new meaning to fast fashion. 10s 10s 10s across the board. Souped up and supercharged, just installed the NOS kit. Try and keep up 🧡

Vixi

Oh, you’ve heard of me? Good. I’m Vixi— the one they love to call the “Villain”. My lashes came to slay so step out my way, my nails cut deeper than your excuses. I’m too busy living in my villain era, let the haters hate. So, what’s it gonna be—friend or foe? Are, you rolling with me or am I running you over?
